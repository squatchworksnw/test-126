# Welcome Email Plan

Do not build a full invite email until the account/invite flow is ready to support it.

## Purpose

The welcome email should help a new person sign in, understand their role, and know who to contact.

## Recommended Content

Subject:

```text
Welcome to SquatchWorks
```

Body should include:

- Role:
  - Owner, Admin, or Submitter.
- App URL:
  - `https://squatchworksnw.github.io/test-126/`
- First steps:
  - Open the app.
  - Use the email address that was granted workspace access.
  - Sign in with password or email sign-in link.
  - If the password is unknown, use Forgot password.
- Admin contact:
  - Workspace administrator name.
  - Support email.
  - Support phone.

## Plain-English Draft

```text
Welcome to SquatchWorks.

Your workspace role is: [role].

Open the app here:
https://squatchworksnw.github.io/test-126/

Use this email address to sign in:
[email]

If you do not know your password, choose Forgot password on the sign-in screen.

For help, contact:
[admin name]
[support email]
[support phone]
```

## Build Later

When the invite/account flow is ready, send this from the trusted account system, not from the public browser app.
